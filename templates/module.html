Overview of Cybersecurity and Cyber Hygiene

Cybersecurity means keeping computers, phones, and other electronic devices safe from bad people who want to steal information or do damage. Cybersecurity is important because we store a lot of personal information on our devices, like our passwords, photos, and messages, and we don't want anyone else to have access to them.

Cyber hygiene is like taking a shower for our electronic devices. Just like we wash our hands and take showers to stay clean and healthy, we need to take good care of our devices to keep them safe and healthy too. Cyber hygiene means doing things like setting strong passwords, not sharing personal information with strangers online, and being careful about what we click on and download.

The goal of cyber hygiene is to make sure our devices and information are safe from bad people. By practicing good cyber hygiene, we can reduce the chances of someone trying to steal our information or do harm to our devices.
